Risk Factors

MDR/Health-care associated pathogens
  1. Broad spectrum antibiotics within 90 days

  2. Hospitalization > 5 days

  3. Local high antibiotic resistance rates

  4. Chronic dialysis within 30 days

  5. Home wound care,family member with MDR infection

  6. Mechanical ventilation =5 days

  7. Immunosuppression

  8. Structural lung disease

  9. IV drug use

Fungemia

  2. Central venous catheter

  3. Parenteral nutrition

  4. Renal replacement therapy in ICU

  5. Neutropenia

  6. Hematologic malignancy

  7. Implantable prosthetic devices

  8. Immunosuppression

  9. Chemotherapy
